About SimpleLife

SimpleLife is an illustrative life-insurance quote demo built to showcase end-to-end agentic development: a real web application, a Claude-powered multi-agent orchestrator that builds and modifies it, and a Playwright test-automation framework the agents drive.

How the quote is calculated

  • Base rate scales with age (starting at $0.50 per $1,000 of coverage).
  • Smokers pay a 1.75× multiplier.
  • Health rating adjusts the premium between 0.85× (excellent) and 1.75× (poor).
  • Longer terms incur a small per-year increment.

None of this is actuarially sound — the numbers are for demonstration only.
